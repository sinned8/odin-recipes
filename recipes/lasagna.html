Lasanga

Description of recipe:

This recipe will show you how to make the best lasagna ever.

Ingredients:

  • Ricotta Cheese
  • Spices
  • Garlic
  • Beef & Sausage
  • Pasta sauce
  • Mozzarella & Parmesan
  • Egg
  • Lasagna noodles
  • Onion
  • Tomato Paste

Recipe:

  1. Boil pasta: Cook in a large pot of salted water.
  2. Meat sauce: Cook sausage and beef with onion and garlic. Drain well, add the pasta sauce & simmer it for a few minutes to thicken.
  3. Combine cheese mixture: Stir the cheese mixture together in a bowl.
  4. Layer & bake: Layer the meat sauce and cheese mixture with lasagna noodles and bake until browned and bubbly.
